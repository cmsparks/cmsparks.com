What I Do

I am passionate about data, app development, and hackathons. For me data visualization is like a puzzle where the pieces are the data and the code. I focus on web based visualizations where I am able to hone my skills in designing ways to display data in a visually appealing way.

Development

This summer, I worked at the startup Spokin fixing bugs on their mobile application. I created a prototype for an electronic version of the Emergency Care Plan, a form used by thousands of allergists. I am also currently working on a moodtracking application that is used internally to track doctor and nurse mood/stress data at the Lurie Children's Hospital in Chicago which will improve patient care.

Hackathons

I organize hackathons for the Chicago coding community. The hackathons that I help organize are more focused on teaching attendees how to code, rather than being focused on comeptition. They have been supported by CS4All, the computer science department for Chicago Public Schools. I also give student-focused insight into computer science curriculum and events on CS4All's Student Advisory Council.
